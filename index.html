Why use the Black Meerkat?
For those willing to undertake the great responsibility of owning these exotic animals, look no further. We ensure that these animals have been minimally handled while within our care and are solely handled for capture and transportation purposes. This allows you, the customer, to have the full experience of owning an exotic, wild animal.
